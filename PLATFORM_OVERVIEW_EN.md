## ğŸ“Š Assessment Methodology

The platform uses a **3-Phase Evaluation System**:

### Phase 1: Group Evaluation
**Focus:** Team collaboration and group performance

- Entire group evaluated as a single unit
- Assessment criteria: Collaboration, Role Distribution, Work Quality
- All team members receive the same group phase score
- Tool: Comprehensive Observation Cards

### Phase 2: Individual Assessment
**Focus:** Individual student competency

- Individual assignments or tasks
- Assesses understanding of the concepts
- Each student receives their own score
- Measures: Problem-solving, Application, Individual Contribution

### Phase 3: Oral Examination
**Focus:** Verbal communication and deep understanding

- Direct conversation with supervisor/instructor
- Questions about concepts and decisions
- Ability to explain and defend ideas
- Each student receives individual score

### Final Grade Calculation

